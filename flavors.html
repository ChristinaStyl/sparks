FLAVORS

Our Signature Collection

Every Sparks flavor has its own personality, color identity and refreshing experience.

Berries Mix

The combination of Berries Mix creates a rich and refreshing flavor experience with sweet and slightly tart berry notes. Its cool fruity profile delivers freshness, balance and natural enjoyment.

Strawberry & Lemon

The combination of Strawberry & Lemon delivers a vibrant and refreshing experience with fruity sweetness and citrus freshness. Strawberry is balanced by energetic lemon acidity.

Mango & Pineapple

The combination of Mango & Pineapple creates an exotic tropical flavor experience full of freshness and energy. The juicy sweetness of mango blends perfectly with pineapple intensity.

Peach & Green Apple

The combination of Peach & Green Apple creates a smooth and refreshing flavor profile with juicy sweetness and crisp freshness. Peach is enhanced by cool green apple sharpness.

Cherry & Vanilla

The combination of Cherry & Vanilla creates a sweet and smooth flavor experience with rich fruity intensity and creamy notes. Cherry blends harmoniously with soft vanilla warmth.

Watermelon & Mint

The combination of Watermelon & Mint creates a cooling and refreshing flavor profile with fruity sweetness and herbal freshness. Watermelon is balanced perfectly by crisp mint.
