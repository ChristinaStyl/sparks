ABOUT SPARKS

Sparks is a modern sparkling water brand created to deliver freshness, energy and natural enjoyment through fruity flavor experiences and premium design.

A New Sparkling Experience

Sparks is an innovative sparkling water brand created to offer refreshing beverages with authentic fruity flavors. The name “Sparks” reflects the feeling of freshness and joy that every sip brings, while the colorful fruity combinations create a unique experience.

Every beverage is vegan, free from preservatives and artificial carbonation, making it ideal for people seeking a healthier and more balanced lifestyle. With every sip, Sparks highlights the freshness and fruity character that revitalizes body and mind.

Goals & Values

Sparks was created to bring a new type of beverage into the market, one that combines freshness, natural ingredients and energy without compromise.

Naturalness

Every flavor is inspired by authentic natural ingredients without artificial taste enhancers.

Wellness

Sparks promotes a refreshing and balanced lifestyle through healthier beverage choices.

Sustainability

The brand respects the environment by avoiding artificial carbonation and unnecessary additives.

Innovation

Unique fruity combinations transform every sip into a refreshing and energetic experience.

Our Message & Story

The story of Sparks began with the need for a sparkling water that combined the freshness of carbonated water with a clean and natural taste experience. Its founders were people who, tired of artificial and often unhealthy soft drinks, decided to create something completely different: a drink that could be vegan, healthy and with a real fruity taste, without harmful ingredients. Thus, Sparks was created with an emphasis on naturalness, transparency and quality, bringing a drink to the market that gives a feeling of freshness and rejuvenation.

Sparks wants to convey the message that healthy drinking can be enjoyable, natural and refreshing at the same time. The product offers a feeling of "freshness and coolness" with fruity flavors that are friendly to the body and the environment. With every sip, Sparks proposes a better way to enjoy sparkling water – with natural vegan ingredients, without preservatives or carbonation, promoting a balanced and modern lifestyle.

Freshness. Energy. Natural Enjoyment.

Sparks' main goal is to bring to the market a drink that offers natural coolness and revitalization, giving a feeling of freshness and energy without harmful substances. Through the use of pure and vegan ingredients, Sparks offers a safe and enjoyable sparkling water option, without carbonation, designed for everyday consumption and ideal for those who wish to escape from the usual, conventional drinks.
