Promotional Collection

Sparks is presented at marathons as a refreshing and revitalizing experience, ideal for those who test their limits. With its premium character, it invites runners to discover an unexpected fruity combination, offering a breath of freshness during or after the race.

At the marathon event, Sparks will be accompanied by thoughtful promotional products such as bags, t-shirts, water bottles, glasses and posters, which convey the message of energy, freshness and natural enjoyment at every moment of the route, offering a unique experience.
